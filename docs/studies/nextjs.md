## O que é layout.tsx?
De acordo com Gemini da pesquisa do google, This is the top-most layout in your application and is required.
It must contain the `<html>` and `<body>` tags, along with the children prop.
It defines the global structure and elements that wrap all other content in your application, like a global header or footer.

## Como fazer Nested Layouts?
De acordo com Gemini da pesquisa do google, You can define layout.tsx files within nested folders to apply specific layouts to particular route segments.
